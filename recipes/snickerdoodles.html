Soft & Thick Snickerdoodles

Description

These soft and thick snickerdoodles are sugar cookies coated in ground cinnamon and granulated sugar.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons cream of tartar
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 1/2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 2/3 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit. Line two cookie sheets with parchment paper and set aside.
  2. In a small bowl, combine 1/3 cup of sugar and 1 teaspoon of cinnamon.
  3. In a medium bowl, whisk the flour, cream of tartar, baking soda, cinnamon, and salt.
  4. In a large bowl, beat the butter and sugar until smooth and creamy. Add the egg, egg yolk, and vanilla extract, and beat until combined. Add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients in three parts.
  5. Roll cookie dough (1.5 tablespoons each) into balls. Coat the balls in cinnamon and sugar. Arrange on the baking sheet.
  6. Bake for 10 minutes.
