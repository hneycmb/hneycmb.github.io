Chocolate Crinkle Cookies

Description

These chocolate crinkle cookies are as rich and fudgy as brownies and are coated in powdered sugar.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup + 2 tablespoons natural unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup powdered sugar

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, beat the butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until fluffy. Add the egg and vanilla extract and beat until combined.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk the flour, cocoa powder, baking soda, and salt until combined. Slowly pour into the wet ingredients and beat until combined.
  3. Cover tightly and chill in the refrigerator for 3 hours.
  4. Let the dough to sit at room temperature for 10 minutes.
  5. Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit. Line baking sheets with parchment paper and set aside.
  6. Roll dough (1.5 tablespoons each) into balls. Coat each ball in powdered sugar. Place on the baking sheets.
  7. Bake for 10-12 minutes.
